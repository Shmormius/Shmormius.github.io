Light Bulb Hot Air Balloons

This render is my attempt at replicating a graphic design from the internet as a 3D environment. The main issues are the skybox and the lightbulb filament. I love using nodes to create textures in Blender so it was fun learning to use that. The filament however was a pain in my a##. The filament was made using a second scene overlaid over the first.
