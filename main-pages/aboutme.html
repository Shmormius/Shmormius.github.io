ETC is a youth volunteer group operated by FYI (family Youth Initiatives). The group aims to create spaces for young people in Salida and advocate for youth mental health and drug abuse. Now You Know 2019-2020

Now You Know was a volunteer group ran be the Alliance in Salida. The Alliance is an organization that aims to help victims of domestic and spousal violence/abuse as well as educate about it. Now you know existed as a mode for Youth to gain more knowledge about domestic abuse. We were also responsible for spreading awareness to our peers. We organized and ran a teen dating abuse week at our local high school that included events, assemblies, and games that educated about the dangers of dating abuse and provided resources to this effect.
